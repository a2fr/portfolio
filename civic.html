Sulitest

Sulitest is a test that helps university students and teachers learn about sustainability, which is the idea of protecting the environment and society for future generations. The test asks questions to see how much people know about sustainability and helps universities teach about it in their courses.

Ideation Day

An ideation day is a day set aside for the purpose of generating ideas. Ideation days are often used in the business or corporate world as a way to encourage creativity and generate new ideas for products, services, or processes.

The goal of the ideation day in ENSEEIHT was to find projects to reduce carbon footprint and energy consumption of the school.

Thus, with seven classmates, we present a new project to the school : Eco-Active. To put in a nutshell, our project is a social network which encourages individuals to do eco-friendly and daily actions, by setting a competitive aspect of those actions. Indeed, the most you respect the environment, and the most you become high-ranked in the network and then the most you will gain rewards (food, electronic supplies, ...).

King-Kong's trial

We attended a play in ENSEEIHT tackling harassment issues. There were three students who were acting as normal students from ENSEEIHT during day and night parties, and also days after parties.
